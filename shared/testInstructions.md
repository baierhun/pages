## **Test Instructions**

Assessments are designed to measure **your individual understanding** of the material. They must be completed **carefully, honestly, and entirely on your own**.

1. ### **Independent Work Only**

   1. Tests are **solo assessments**.  
   2. You may **NOT** communicate with anyone while taking a test.  
   3. No talking, No whispering, No messaging, No signaling or sharing screens  
   4. Your answers must reflect **your own thinking**, not anyone else’s.

2. ### **No Outside Resources Allowed**    You may **NOT** use *any* resources while taking a test, including but not limited to:

   1. Notes (paper or digital)  
   2. Slides, worksheets, or previous assignments  
   3. Textbooks or reference books  
   4. Websites, search engines, tutorials, or videos  
   5. AI tools or assistants of any kind  
   6. Code examples, past solutions, or saved files

**The only thing you should have open is the test itself.**  
If you are unsure whether something counts as a resource, **assume it is NOT allowed**.

3. ### **Test Security and Confidentiality**

   1. All test materials are confidential. You may NOT remove, copy, record, save, photograph, screenshot, or reproduce any part of a test.  
   2. You may NOT share test content with anyone at any time, including:  
      1. Sending or posting questions  
      2. Describing questions from memory  
      3. Messaging classmates about specific problems  
      4. Saving questions, prompts, or answers for later use  
   3. This applies during and after the test.

   Once the test is completed, the questions remain the intellectual property of the instructor and may not be reused or redistributed in any form.

   Violating test security compromises the integrity of the assessment and will be treated as an academic integrity violation.

4. ### **Technology Rules**

   1. Only the software or tools explicitly provided as part of the test may be used.  
   2. Do not open additional tabs, windows, or applications unless instructed.  
   3. Do not copy, paste, or share any part of the test.  
   4. Any attempt to bypass restrictions or access outside help will be treated as a violation.

5. ### **Prior Access to Test Information**

   1. You may **NOT** receive, request, or use any information about a test **before** taking it.  
   2. This includes:  
      1. Seeing the test ahead of time  
      2. Knowing specific questions or answers in advance  
      3. Being told what topics, problems, or formats will appear beyond what was announced to the entire class  
      4. Receiving hints, summaries, screenshots, or explanations from other students who have already taken the test  
   3. If you have prior knowledge of specific test questions or content, you are expected to **inform the instructor immediately** before beginning the test.

   Using advanced knowledge of the test gives an unfair advantage and is considered a violation of academic integrity, even if no resources are used during the test itself.

6. ### **Work Carefully and Thoughtfully**

   1. Read **every question completely** before answering.  
   2. Pay close attention to:  
      1. Instructions, Keywords, Required formats  
   3. Rushing increases mistakes. Take your time.  
   4. Make sure your answers are **clear, complete, and intentional**.

7. ### **Academic Integrity**    By taking a test, you are confirming that:

   1. All answers are **your own work**  
   2. You followed all rules listed above  
   3. You did not receive help from any person or resource

   Any violation of these rules may result in:

   1. Loss of credit  
   2. A failing grade on the assessment  
   3. Further academic consequences according to school policy

8. ### **Bathroom Breaks**

   1. Please use the bathroom before the test

9. ### **Questions During the Test**

   1. If something is unclear due to a **typo or technical issue**, raise your hand or notify the instructor.  
   2. The instructor **will not** explain concepts or give hints during the test.

10. ### **Final Check Before Submitting**

    Before submitting:  
    1. Review your answers  
    2. Make sure you responded to **every question**  
    3. Confirm that you followed all instructions

    Once submitted, answers cannot be changed.
