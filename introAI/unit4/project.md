### **Your Presentation Should Include**

1. **Introduction**

   * What is your AI technique?

   * Generally, what is its purpose?

   * Discuss how game genres and components relate to this technique  
     1. What genres would incorporate this technique? Why? How?  
     2. Are there any components that use or are used by this technique?

2. **How It Works**

   * Explain the logic or system behind it (visually if possible).

   * Include a simple example (diagram, flowchart, or short scenario).

   * You must explain this yourself. Do not rely on a video to explain your topic.

3. **Real Game Example**

   * Identify at least one game that uses this technique.

   * Describe how it improves gameplay or player experience.

4. **Visual Aid**

   * Use a slideshow
    * include diagrams or a demo video.

   * Keep it simple and focused—support your explanation, don’t overwhelm it.

   * If you use a video, it cannot explain your topic. It should be used as an example only.

5. **Test Questions**

    * Write three test questions that assess understanding of your presentation. Make a slide for each question.
    * Each question should include:
        * The question itself (multiple choice, fill in the blank, or true/false)
        * Four possible answer choices if multiple choice or fill in the blank (clearly mark the correct one)
        * A brief explanation of the correct answer

6. **Team Work**

   * You must work together on your project.  
   * Everyone must contribute content as equally as possible.  
   * Each person must present as equally as possible.
