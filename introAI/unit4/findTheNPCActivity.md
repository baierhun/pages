# **Find the NPC**

**Instructions:**

You will spend **15–20 minutes** exploring online games. Try **2–3 different games** and identify **three NPCs (non-player characters)**—anything controlled by the computer, such as characters you talk to, enemies, helpers, or background characters. As you play, focus on what each NPC does, how it behaves, how the player interacts with it, and how it adds to the game. Complete the worksheet using your observations and be ready to briefly share one NPC and why it is important to the game.
