## Debate Make-Up Assignment

**Topic:** *Resolved: Machines Can Be Intelligent*

Since you will be absent on the day of the debate, you will complete this assignment instead of serving as the moderator.

### Part 1: Moderator Questions

Write **5 thoughtful questions** that a moderator could ask during the debate.

Your questions should:

* Encourage deeper thinking and discussion
* Challenge the debaters to explain or defend their ideas
* Avoid simple **yes/no** answers

At least:

* **2 questions should challenge the “For” side**
* **2 questions should challenge the “Against” side**
* **1 question should encourage both sides to respond to each other**

---

### Part 2: Argument Essay – Supporting the Resolution

Write a **short essay (1–2 paragraphs)** arguing **in favor of the resolution**:
*Machines can be intelligent.*

Your response should:

* Clearly state the argument
* Provide reasoning or examples
* Explain why machines should be considered intelligent

---

### Part 3: Argument Essay – Opposing the Resolution

Write a **short essay (1–2 paragraphs)** arguing **against the resolution**:
*Machines can be intelligent.*

Your response should:

* Clearly state the argument
* Provide reasoning or examples
* Explain why machines should **not** be considered intelligent
