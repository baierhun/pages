# **Debate Instructions**

**Resolution:** *Machines can be intelligent*
**Teams:** 2 teams of 4 students
**Moderators:** 2 students
**Total Time:** 50 minutes (Debate ~40 minutes)

---

## **Roles & Responsibilities**

### **Team Members (Affirmative / Negative)**

* Work with your team to plan arguments.
* Decide on the order of speakers.
* Support your team during rebuttals and cross-examination.

**During Debate:**

1. **Opening Statement (4 min per team)**

   * Each team presents key arguments (2–3 main points).
   * Focus on clarity, examples, and logic.

2. **Rebuttal (4 min per team)**

   * Respond to the opposing team’s arguments.
   * Use evidence and reasoning to refute points.

3. **Cross-Examination / Q&A (5 min per team)**

   * Answer questions from moderators.
   * Be concise and respectful.
   * Support your answers with evidence when possible.

4. **Closing Statement (3 min per team)**

   * Summarize your strongest points.
   * Address the opponent’s key arguments.
   * End with a memorable point.

---

### **Moderators**

* Keep time for all sections of the debate.
* Ask 2–3 questions to each team during cross-examination.
* Ensure all team members speak and follow the rules.
* Help resolve any disputes or confusion during the debate.

**During Debate:**

1. **Opening Instructions**

   * Explain rules and time limits.
   * Confirm speaking order for each team.

2. **Cross-Examination / Q&A**

   * Ask clear questions that challenge each team’s arguments.
   * Limit answers to ~1 minute per student.

3. **Timekeeping & Wrap-Up**

   * Ensure debate stays on schedule.
   * Lead reflection or class vote at the end.

---

## **Debate Schedule (Approx. 40 minutes)**

| Segment                 | Duration | Notes                    |
| ----------------------- | -------- | ------------------------ |
| Setup & instructions    | 5 min    | Moderators explain rules |
| Opening Statements      | 8 min    | 4 min per team           |
| Rebuttals               | 8 min    | 4 min per team           |
| Cross-Examination / Q&A | 10 min   | 5 min per team           |
| Closing Statements      | 6 min    | 3 min per team           |
| Reflection & wrap-up    | 3 min    | Class vote or discussion |
