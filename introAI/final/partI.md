# ✍️ Part I: Story Creation & Presentation

## 📖 Your Task

You will create an original **4-7 page story** using AI as a tool to help you develop ideas, structure, and writing.

> Use the template in this assignment to complete this assignment

> * Submit the google doc **and** share a link in the links document on Canvas  
> * Make sure everyone has **edit** access to your document. If your peers cannot edit your document, your assignment will be considered late until you fix it.

Your story should include:

* A main character
* A clear conflict (problem)
* A resolution (ending)
* A theme or message

---

## 🤖 Using AI

You are **required** to use AI to help you write your story—but you are still responsible for the quality.

### Requirements:

* You must include **at least 3 prompts** that you used
* You are encouraged to use more prompts to improve your story
* Your prompts should show **progression** (not repeating the same thing)

---

## 🧠 Your Responsibility

AI is a starting point, not the final product.

You should:

* Review what the AI generates
* Make changes and improvements
* Ensure your story makes sense and is complete

---

## 🗣️ Presentation

You will present your story to the class in a **1–2 minute summary**.

### Your presentation must include:

* A brief overview of your story
* The main character and conflict
* How your story ends
* An explanation of your **process**, including:

  * How you used AI
  * How your prompts changed or improved your story

---

## 💬 Peer Feedback

>⚠️ You may **not** use AI to help in any way to give feedback

You will give **3 pieces of feedback** to **2 different students**.

Every document has a section for feedback. You man **not** give feedback on work that already has 2 pieces of feedback

### Your comments can address:

* **clarity**

  * What was confusing or unclear?
* **engagement**

  * Where did the story get boring, slow, or less interesting?
* **improvement**

  * What is one specific suggestion to make the story better?

👉 Each student will give a total of **6 pieces of feedback** (3 per story × 2 students)

Be specific and helpful—this feedback will be used for revision.

---

## 📦 What to Submit

### 1. Your Story

* Minimum **4 pages**
* Maximum **7 pages**

### 2. Your Prompt Log

* At least **3 prompts**
* Include the AI responses (or key parts of them)

---

## ⚠️ Important

* Do not rely on a single prompt
* Do not submit the first thing AI gives you
* You are responsible for making sure your story is clear, complete, and meaningful

---

## 💡 Tip

If your story feels boring, confusing, or incomplete, that’s a sign you need to:

* Ask better prompts
* Revise what AI gave you
* Take control of the story yourself

AI helps—but **you are the author**.
