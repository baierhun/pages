# 🗣️ Part II: Story Presentation, Feedback, & Revision

## 🎯 Today’s Goal

Today you will:

1. Give and receive feedback
2. Begin revising your story

---

## 💬 Part 2: Peer Feedback

You will give feedback to **2 classmates**.

### For EACH classmate, provide:

* **Clarity:** What was confusing or unclear?
* **Engagement:** Where did it get boring or slow?
* **Improvement:** One specific suggestion to make it better

👉 Total: **6 pieces of feedback**

Be honest, specific, and helpful—your classmates will use this to improve their work.

---

## 🧠 Part 3: Review Your Feedback

After receiving feedback, review what others said about your story.

### Identify:

* Common issues (things multiple people mentioned)
* The most helpful suggestions

---

## 🔄 Part 4: Required Revisions

Revise your story using your peer feedback. You must:

* Use **at least 3 pieces of feedback**
* Clearly show:

  * What the feedback was
  * What you changed in your story

---

## 📦 What to Submit

1. **Feedback You Gave**

   * 2 students
   * 3 comments each (6 total)

   > Copy and paste all of your comments and submit them on this assignment on Canvas

2. **Revisions**

    * In your story document, make the necessary revisions to your story based on the peer reviews. 

---

## ⚠️ Important

* You must use feedback to improve your story
