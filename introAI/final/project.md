# 🎬 AI Creative Studio Project

## Overview

In this project, you will use AI as a **tool** (not a shortcut) to create something meaningful. You have already written a story, now you'll turn that story into a creative product.

You will choose **one** of the following:

* 📖 Comic Book
* 🎮 Online Game
* 🎵 Music Video
* 🎬 Short Film
* 🎭 Audio Drama

---

## 🧠 Project Goal

Learn how to:

* Work with AI to create ideas
* Improve weak AI-generated content
* Turn a written story into a finished creative product

---

## 📅 Timeline

### **Day 1 – Story Creation**

You have created a story using AI *and your own ideas*.

---

### **Day 2 – Presentation + Feedback**

You already:

1. Presented your story
2. Received feedback from classmates

---

### **Days 3–5 – Create Your Project**

You will turn your story into ONE of the following:

---

## 📖 Comic Book

* 10 pages minimum with 6-10 panels per page
* AI can generate individual panels, but not entire pages
* Dialogue and visuals
* Clear beginning, middle, end

---

## 🎮 Online Game

* Player interaction
* A goal or scoring system
* Integrated story

---

## 🎵 Music Video

* Song or lyrics connected to your story
* Visual component (video, animation, or slideshow)
* Clear meaning or message

---

## 🎬 Short Film

* Script
* At least 3 scenes
* 2-5 minutes long
* Intentional camera shots or visuals

---

## 🎭 Audio Drama

* Bring the story to life
* Include background music, characters, scenes
* **Not** an audio book/narration of your story

## 🔒 Important Rules

* You **must use your story** as the foundation
* You **must revise your story** before building
* You **must use AI more than once** (not just a single prompt)

---

## 🧩 Final Submission

You will turn in:

### 1. Final Project

Your comic, game, video, or film

### 2. Story + Revisions

* Original story
* Updated version with changes

---

## 💡 Final Reminder

AI can help you start—but it’s your job to **improve, adapt, and create something meaningful**.

If you rely on one prompt, your project will not be strong enough.
