## **Final Exam Overview**

This **final exam** assesses your understanding of key concepts from **Units 1–3 in CS Academy**, along with topics from the **Mini-Module**. The exam is divided into **three sections**:

### **1\. Multiple Choice**

* **15 questions**  
* Covers material from **Units 1–3**  
* Questions are similar in style and difficulty to the **unit tests** you have taken throughout the year

### **2\. Matching**

* **10 terms or concepts** from **Units 1–3**  
* **10 descriptions**  
* You will match each term or concept to its correct description

### **3\. Short Answer**

* **3 questions** based on the Mini-Module topics:  
  * Computer ethics  
  * Cyber safety (Phishing)
  * Safety on social media

### **How to Study**

To prepare for the final exam, you may review:

* Your **notes, slides, overviews, and checkpoints** from Units 1–3  
* The **slides and exercises** from the Mini-Module  
* Previous tests \- in class only

Take your time, read each question carefully, and do your best to clearly show what you know.
