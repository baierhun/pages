# **Raspberry Pi GPIO Assignment**

## **Overview**

You will use GPIO pins on a Raspberry Pi to control an LED.

## **Part I – Blinking LED**

Create a circuit and program that makes an LED blink continuously.

### **Requirements**

* LED turns on and off repeatedly
* Uses a GPIO pin
* Includes a delay

### **Checkpoint**

* You must **demo your working LED** to me before moving on
