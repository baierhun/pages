## Ridiculous Case Courtroom

# 🐒 The Monkey Selfie

**Incident:**  
A monkey used a tourist’s camera to take a photo that later became popular. The tourist sold the image.

**Defendant:** The tourist  
**Plaintiff:** Wildlife advocacy group  
**Charge:** Unjust Enrichment  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The monkey took the photo  
- The tourist owned the camera  
- The tourist profited from the image  

---

# 🚗 Self-Driving Crash

**Incident:**  
A self-driving car ran a red light and caused a crash while in autonomous mode.

**Defendant:** The human driver  
**Plaintiff:** Other driver  
**Charge:** Negligence  

**Agreed Facts:**
- Car was in autonomous mode  
- Driver was not controlling it  
- The car caused the crash  

---

# 🐕 The Parrot Witness

**Incident:**  
A parrot present during an incident repeatedly says “Don’t do it!” One side wants to use this as evidence.

**Issue:** Should this be allowed as evidence?  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The parrot says the phrase clearly  
- It was present during the incident  
- It’s unknown when it learned the phrase  

---

# 🍔 Too-Hot Food Case

**Incident:**  
A customer spilled extremely hot food and was burned.

**Defendant:** The restaurant  
**Plaintiff:** The customer  
**Charge:** Negligence  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The food was very hot  
- The customer spilled it  
- No warning was given  

---

# 🎨 Accidental Art Disposal

**Incident:**  
A cleaner threw away materials that turned out to be a valuable art piece.

**Defendant:** The cleaner  
**Plaintiff:** The artist  
**Charge:** Destruction of Property  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The artwork looked like trash  
- It was in a shared space  
- The cleaner was doing their job  

---

# 🧠 Memory Loss Case

**Incident:**  
A person committed an act but claims they don’t remember it.

**Defendant:** The person  
**Plaintiff:** The state  
**Charge:** Criminal act (focus on intent)  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The act occurred  
- The defendant claims memory loss  
- No proof whether memory loss is real  

---

# 🛍️ The Wallet

**Incident:**  
A person found a wallet with cash and ID and kept it.

**Defendant:** The finder  
**Plaintiff:** Wallet owner / State  
**Charge:** Theft  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The wallet had ID  
- The finder kept it  
- No attempt to return it  

---

# 🎥 Viral Video Case

**Incident:**  
A person filmed someone in public and posted it online. It went viral and caused embarrassment.

**Defendant:** The person who filmed  
**Plaintiff:** The person filmed  
**Charge:** Invasion of Privacy / Harm  

**Agreed Facts:**
- Filmed in public  
- No consent  
- It caused embarrassment  

---

# 🧪 Experiment Gone Wrong

**Incident:**  
A scientist followed proper procedures, but an experiment caused damage.

**Defendant:** The scientist  
**Plaintiff:** Affected parties  
**Charge:** Negligence  

**Agreed Facts:**
- Procedures were followed  
- Outcome was unexpected  
- Damage occurred  

---

# 🚪 The Broken Door

**Incident:**  
A person broke a door to escape a building they believed was dangerous. There was no real danger.

**Defendant:** The person  
**Plaintiff:** Property owner  
**Charge:** Destruction of Property  

**Agreed Facts:**
- The door was broken  
- The person believed there was danger  
- There was no actual danger
